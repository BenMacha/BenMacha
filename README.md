## About Me

Experienced **Tech Lead & Full Stack Developer** based in **Paris, France** with 8+ years building scalable web and mobile applications. I specialize in **Symfony, React, and Vue.js** with deep expertise in system architecture, real-time platforms, and team leadership.

Currently **IT Project Manager at ORPI**, focusing on data-driven dashboard development, GDPR-compliant data processing, and technical innovation through AI and automation. Previously spent 6 years as **Technical Lead at Keytchens**, where I led a major Symfony migration (v2.8 to v6.4) without service interruption and built real-time order management systems integrating Uber Eats and Deliveroo APIs.

I'm passionate about **open source** and actively contribute Symfony bundles and SDKs to the community.
